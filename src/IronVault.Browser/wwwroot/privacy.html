IRON VAULT PRIVACY POLICY

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: 2025-01-01  |  Last updated: 2026-03-20

Iron Vault is a fully offline, single-player game. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data — ever.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no information whatsoever. Iron Vault does not require an account, does not contact any server, and does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services.

2. Data Storage

All game state (such as selected difficulty or level) exists only in local application memory for the duration of a single session. No data is written to disk, cloud storage, or any external service.

3. Network Access

The game operates entirely offline. It makes zero network requests during gameplay. The browser / WebAssembly version loads static files from the host server once on startup; no further communication occurs after the page is loaded.

4. Permissions

Iron Vault requests no sensitive device permissions. Specifically, it does not access:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Files or photos outside the application sandbox
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Biometric or health data

5. Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data at all, Iron Vault is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13 (COPPA) and under 16 (GDPR). No parental consent is required for data processing, as no processing takes place.

6. Third-Party Services

Iron Vault integrates no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporters, or analytics services. No data is shared with any third party.

7. Your Rights

Since we hold no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete. No data subject request is necessary or applicable.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Given the nature of the application, we do not anticipate material changes.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you are welcome to open an issue on the project repository:
